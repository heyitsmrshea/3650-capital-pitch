04 / Structure

How we structure
every transaction.

Our disciplined approach combines rigorous credit analysis with creative structuring to deliver certainty of execution for every borrower.

Phase 01

Origination &
Underwriting

Our originations team works directly with sponsors to understand the business plan, evaluate the asset, and structure a loan that aligns with the project's trajectory. Local market expertise ensures informed credit decisions.

Phase 02

Closing &
Funding

As a balance sheet lender, we control the closing process end-to-end. No syndication risk. No last-minute committee surprises. When we commit, we close. Typical timelines run 30 to 45 days from term sheet to funding.

Phase 03

Servicing &
Asset Management

Post-closing, our in-house servicing team becomes the borrower's permanent point of contact. Draw requests, modifications, and extensions are handled internally with decision-making authority that third-party servicers lack.
