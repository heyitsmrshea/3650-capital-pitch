01 / The Firm

One lender.
Wholly committed.

3650 Capital is a vertically integrated balance sheet lender built to provide borrowers with a single, reliable lending relationship from origination to maturity.

02 / Ethos

Built on principles
that endure.

We established 3650 Capital to solve a fundamental flaw in commercial real estate lending: the absence of continuity. Every decision we make is guided by four foundational pillars.

01

Continuity of
relationship

Borrowers deserve a lender who stays with them across the entire lifecycle of a loan. We do not originate and sell. We originate, service, and manage every loan we make.

02

Vertical
integration

From origination through asset management, special servicing, and CMBS issuance, every function operates under one roof. No handoffs. No fragmentation.

03

Institutional
discipline

Our credit committee has decades of combined experience across multiple market cycles. We apply rigorous underwriting while maintaining the agility of a purpose-built platform.

04

Alignment of
interests

As a balance sheet lender, our capital is permanently aligned with our borrowers' success. When a borrower wins, we win. There is no misalignment in our model.

03 / Platform

The architecture
of certainty.

Our platform was engineered to eliminate the inefficiencies that plague traditional CRE lending. Every capability exists in-house. Every decision is made with complete information.

01

Origination

Seven offices nationwide. Local market expertise with national scale. Direct relationships with sponsors and developers across every major market.

02

Servicing

In-house master and special servicing ensures that borrowers interact with the same team from day one through loan payoff. No third-party handoffs.

03

Capital Markets

Proprietary CMBS issuance capability allows us to efficiently recycle capital while retaining loan servicing and borrower relationships permanently.
