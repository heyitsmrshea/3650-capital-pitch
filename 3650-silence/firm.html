Philosophy

Built for permanence,
not exits.

Most commercial real estate lenders operate on a fund-cycle timeline. They originate, securitize, and move on. The borrower becomes someone else's problem.

3650 Capital was structured differently from inception. We are balance-sheet lenders with permanent capital -- no fund expirations, no forced dispositions, no incentive misalignment. When we make a commitment, we honor it through the full lifecycle of the asset.

This is not a strategy. It is our identity.

The Platform

How the platform operates.

Our platform is engineered for vertical integration. Origination, underwriting, closing, servicing, and asset management all operate under one roof, providing borrowers with a single point of accountability.

This architecture eliminates the friction, delays, and misaligned incentives that plague traditional lending models. When a borrower needs a decision, they get one -- from the same team that made the original commitment.
