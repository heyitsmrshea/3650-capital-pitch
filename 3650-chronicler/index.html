The Operating Thesis

Vertical integration
as a lending discipline.

Most commercial real estate lenders originate loans and then hand them off. The originator moves on. The servicer inherits a file. The borrower inherits a stranger. Misalignment is structural.

3650 Capital was built on a different premise: the lender who originates should also structure, service, and asset-manage. When every discipline operates under one roof, information flows without friction, decisions are made in context, and borrowers deal with the same team from the first conversation through the final payoff.

This is not a philosophy. It is an operating system — one that produces faster execution, better outcomes, and genuine continuity of relationship across the full ten-year lifecycle of a loan.

Capabilities

How the platform operates.

handshake 01 — Origination

Direct lending relationships

We originate bridge, permanent, and mezzanine loans directly with borrowers and sponsors. No intermediary layers. One point of contact from term sheet through closing.

architecture 02 — Structuring

Bespoke capital solutions

Every loan is structured to the specific needs of the asset, the business plan, and the borrower. Our structuring team works alongside origination to design capital that fits — not capital that approximates.

monitoring 03 — Servicing

In-house loan administration

We service every loan we originate. The servicing team sits with the originators. Borrower requests are handled by people who know the deal, the sponsor, and the intent behind the structure.

analytics 04 — Asset Management

Proactive portfolio oversight

Our asset management team monitors each loan through its lifecycle — anticipating needs, resolving issues before they escalate, and ensuring the business plan proceeds as agreed.

3650
Our Name

From day one
to day 3,650.

A standard commercial real estate loan has a ten-year term. Ten years equals 3,650 days. That number is not accidental — it is the organizing principle of everything we do.

Most lenders are optimized for the first ninety days: origination, closing, securitization. We built a firm optimized for all 3,650. Our platform is designed so that the borrower who signs the term sheet on day one is working with the same team on day 3,000.

The name is a commitment to permanence. It means we do not disappear after closing. We do not transfer your loan to a stranger. We remain present, informed, and aligned for the full lifecycle of the capital relationship.
