Founders

Co-Founders

The founding partners who built 3650 Capital from the ground up.

Justin Kennedy

Co-Founder, Managing Partner

3650 Capital Co-Founder Justin Kennedy brings 32 years of CRE experience, including finance, distressed assets and development. In 2013, Kennedy and Cobb co-founded 3650 Capital's CRE development affiliate, Grass River Property, a leading South Florida developer and manager of mixed-use, urban-infill and transit-oriented properties. In partnership with leading institutional partners, the Grass River portfolio includes 14 development and equity investments totaling more than 1.8 million SF.

Prior to Grass River, Kennedy and Cobb served as Co-CEOs of LNR Property LLC. Mr. Kennedy led the firm's strategic direction and investment activities across LNR's global CRE debt, equity and distressed assets platforms. Prior to LNR, Mr. Kennedy served 11 years at Deutsche Bank as Managing Director and Global Head of Real Estate Capital Markets. Prior to Deutsche Bank, Mr. Kennedy was Head of CMBS Trading at Goldman Sachs.

Mr. Kennedy holds an A.B. in Economics from Stanford University and an M.B.A. from UCLA.

Jonathan Roth

Co-Founder, Managing Partner

Mr. Roth brings 30 years of experience originating, structuring and servicing CRE investments. Previously, Mr. Roth served as President of Canyon Partners Real Estate LLC. During his 18-year tenure at Canyon, Mr. Roth was instrumental in the formation of several investment vehicles which collectively invested approximately $5.6 billion in over 200 transactions.

Prior to joining Canyon, Mr. Roth was a partner at the international law firm of Loeb & Loeb. Mr. Roth currently sits on the Board of Governors for Cedars Sinai Hospital and the Advisory Board of the Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center.

Mr. Roth holds a B.A. in English Literature from UCLA, and a J.D. from Loyola Law School.

Toby Cobb

Co-Founder, Managing Partner

Toby Cobb has 30 years of experience in commercial real estate and finance. In 2013, Mr. Cobb and Justin Kennedy co-founded Grass River Property, a leading South Florida developer with approximately 2 million square feet of real estate owned.

Prior to Grass River, Mr. Cobb and Mr. Kennedy were Co-CEOs leading the restructuring and eventual sale of LNR Property, LLC. Mr. Cobb built and led LNR's loan origination platform and its primary loan servicing and distressed assets servicing platforms. Prior to LNR, Mr. Cobb was Managing Director and Co-Head of US Commercial Real Estate at Deutsche Bank.

Mr. Cobb holds a B.S. from Southern Methodist University and an M.B.A. from New York University. He is Chairman Emeritus at the University of Miami's Master of Real Estate Development program and a Trustee of the Urban Land Institute.
