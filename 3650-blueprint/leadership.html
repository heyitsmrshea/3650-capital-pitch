Co-Founder

Justin Kennedy

Managing Partner

3650 Capital Co-Founder Justin Kennedy brings 32 years of CRE experience. In 2013, Kennedy and Cobb co-founded Grass River Property, a leading South Florida developer with 14 development and equity investments totaling more than 1.8 million SF. Prior to Grass River, Kennedy and Cobb served as Co-CEOs of LNR Property LLC.

Mr. Kennedy served 11 years at Deutsche Bank as Managing Director and Global Head of Real Estate Capital Markets. Prior to Deutsche Bank, Mr. Kennedy was Head of CMBS Trading at Goldman Sachs. He holds an A.B. in Economics from Stanford University and an M.B.A. from UCLA.

PERS_02
Co-Founder

Jonathan Roth

Managing Partner

Mr. Roth brings 30 years of experience originating, structuring and servicing CRE investments. He served as President of Canyon Partners Real Estate LLC where he was instrumental in the formation of vehicles which collectively invested approximately $5.6 billion in over 200 transactions. Prior to Canyon, Mr. Roth was a partner at Loeb & Loeb.

He sits on the Board of Governors for Cedars Sinai Hospital. He holds a B.A. from UCLA and a J.D. from Loyola Law School.

PERS_03
Co-Founder

Toby Cobb

Managing Partner

Toby Cobb has 30 years of experience in commercial real estate and finance. In 2013, he and Justin Kennedy co-founded Grass River Property with approximately 2 million square feet of real estate owned. Previously they were Co-CEOs of LNR Property, LLC. Mr. Cobb built and led LNR's loan origination and servicing platforms.

Prior to LNR, he was Co-Head of US Commercial Real Estate at Deutsche Bank. He holds a B.S. from SMU and an M.B.A. from NYU. He is a Trustee of the Urban Land Institute.
