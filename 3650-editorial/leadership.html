Justin Kennedy

“Our structure ensures that the person you meet on day one is the same person managing your loan on day 3,650.”

Justin Kennedy is a Co-Founder and Managing Partner of 3650 Capital. He brings decades of experience in commercial real estate finance, with deep expertise spanning origination, structuring, and capital markets execution.

Justin has been instrumental in building 3650 Capital into a vertically integrated lending platform that prioritizes borrower relationships and long-term alignment. His vision for a single-point-of-contact lending model has defined the firm's market position and culture.

Co-Founder, Managing Partner

Jonathan Roth

“Discipline in underwriting is not a constraint — it is the foundation of every lasting borrower relationship we build.”

Jonathan Roth is a Co-Founder and Managing Partner of 3650 Capital. With extensive experience across credit underwriting, portfolio management, and institutional lending, Jonathan oversees the firm's credit and risk management functions.

His disciplined approach to real estate lending has been central to the firm's track record of consistent performance. Jonathan's emphasis on rigorous due diligence and portfolio construction has allowed 3650 Capital to navigate every market cycle with resilience.

“Permanence is our competitive advantage. We do not sell loans — we build partnerships that endure.”

Toby Cobb is a Co-Founder and Managing Partner of 3650 Capital. A veteran of the commercial real estate industry, Toby leads the firm's origination and capital formation efforts.
