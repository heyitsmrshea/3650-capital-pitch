Chapter I

The Origination

Every relationship begins with a conversation. Our origination teams are embedded in local markets — Miami, New York, Dallas, Los Angeles, Chicago, Atlanta, Denver, and Washington D.C. They know the borrowers, the buildings, and the basis.

We underwrite with conviction because we hold what we make. Bridge loans from $10M to $300M+. Permanent loans through our CMBS platform. Mezzanine and preferred equity for the right structures.

Chapter II

The Midpoint

Once the deal closes, most lenders disappear. At 3650, the relationship deepens. Our in-house servicing team becomes the borrower's ongoing point of contact — handling draws, modifications, and everything in between.

It's the mid-life of the loan where trust is built or broken. We choose to build.

Chapter III

The Full Cycle

Maturity. Refinance. Payoff. Or recapitalization. Because we hold our loans and service them in-house, we can offer a seamless path from inception through resolution — and back again.

The name "3650" represents ten years — one full cycle. We are built to be there for the entire journey, not just the beginning.
